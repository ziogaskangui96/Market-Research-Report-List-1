<p><p>Straw blankets are erosion control products used in landscaping and construction to stabilize soil. The Single Net Straw Blanket features a single layer of straw held together by a netting on one side, providing basic protection and moisture retention. In contrast, the Double Net Straw Blanket has two layers of straw sandwiched between nets on both sides, offering enhanced stability and resistance to erosion. Both types promote vegetation growth and protect against soil erosion in various environments.</p></p>
