<p><p>The Copper CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Slurry market includes two primary types: Cu CMP Slurry and Cu Barrier CMP Slurry. Cu CMP Slurry is designed for polishing copper layers in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring smooth and planar surfaces. Cu Barrier CMP Slurry is used for polishing both copper and its barrier layers, such as tantalum or tantalum nitride, to prevent copper diffusion. Both slurries are critical in advanced chip fabrication, enhancing device performance and reliability by enabling precise material removal and surface uniformity.</p></p>
