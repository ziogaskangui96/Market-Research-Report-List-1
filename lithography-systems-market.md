<p><strong>The Lithography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Printers</li><li>Proximity Printers</li><li>Projection Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithography systems encompass different market types based on their printing techniques. Contact printers create patterns by direct contact with the photoresist-coated substrate, offering high resolution but limited to thin layers. Proximity printers use a gap to create patterns, making them more versatile but potentially affecting resolution. Projection printers utilize optical projection to transfer high-density images over larger areas, allowing for faster processing and enhanced scalability. Each type serves unique applications across industries such as electronics, semiconductors, and materials science.</p></p>
