<p><ul><li>Colorimetric</li><li>Fluorometric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lactose Assay Kit market is primarily segmented into two types: colorimetric and fluorometric assays. Colorimetric assays utilize a color change that corresponds to lactose concentration, allowing for easy visual detection and quantification using spectrophotometers. In contrast, fluorometric assays measure changes in fluorescence intensity, providing higher sensitivity and specificity for lactose detection. Both methods are widely used in food and beverage industries, as well as in research laboratories, to ensure lactose content meets quality and regulatory standards.</p></p>
