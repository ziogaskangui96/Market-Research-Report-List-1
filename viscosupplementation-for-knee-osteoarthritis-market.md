<p><p>Viscosupplementation is a medical procedure used to treat knee osteoarthritis by injecting hyaluronic acid (HA) into the joint space. This therapy aims to restore the viscoelastic properties of synovial fluid, improving joint lubrication, reducing pain, and enhancing mobility in patients with osteoarthritis. As the aging population continues to expand and awareness of osteoarthritis treatment options rises, the Viscosupplementation for Knee Osteoarthritis Market is experiencing significant growth. </p><p>The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as increasing prevalence of knee osteoarthritis, advancements in HA formulations, and a growing preference for minimally invasive procedures. Innovations in viscosupplements, including combination therapies and long-lasting formulations, are also shaping market dynamics. Additionally, regulatory approvals and clinical endorsements are enhancing physician confidence in these treatments, further propelling market growth. The rise in healthcare expenditure and the growing number of clinics specializing in joint treatments contribute to the overall expansion of the market, establishing viscosupplementation as a viable and effective option for managing knee osteoarthritis symptoms.</p></p>
