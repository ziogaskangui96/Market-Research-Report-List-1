<p><strong>The Biotextiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-bioabsorbable</li><li>Bioabsorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biotextiles market is categorized into two main types: non-bioabsorbable and bioabsorbable materials. Non-bioabsorbable biotextiles are designed for long-lasting applications, remaining intact within the body or environment, ideal for uses like support structures and permanent implants. In contrast, bioabsorbable biotextiles gradually dissolve over time, integrating with biological processes, making them suitable for temporary applications such as wound healing and tissue engineering, where they promote healing and eliminate the need for surgical removal.</p></p>
