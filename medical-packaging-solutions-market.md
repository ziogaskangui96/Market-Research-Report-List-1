<p><strong>The Medical Packaging Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical packaging solutions market comprises two primary types: primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging involves direct contact with medical products, ensuring safety and sterility. It includes items like vials, syringes, and blister packs designed to protect and preserve the integrity of the contents. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, provides additional protection and branding for primary packaging. This includes boxes, cartons, and protective wraps that facilitate storage, shipping, and inventory management, enhancing visibility and compliance in healthcare settings.</p></p>
