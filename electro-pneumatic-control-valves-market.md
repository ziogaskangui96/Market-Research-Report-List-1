<p><p>The electro-pneumatic control valves market includes various types such as ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, plug valves, piston valves, and diaphragm valves. Ball valves provide quick shut-off, while butterfly valves are space-efficient and suitable for flow regulation. Gate valves are primarily used for on-off control, whereas plug valves allow for easy flow direction changes. Piston valves offer precision in flow control with their cylindrical design, and diaphragm valves are ideal for handling corrosive fluids, ensuring reliable sealing and minimal leakage.</p></p>
