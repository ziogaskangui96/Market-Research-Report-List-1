<p><p>The glass sheets market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. Aluminosilicate glass offers enhanced thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for demanding environments. Borosilicate glass is known for its low thermal expansion, suitable for laboratory glassware. Glass ceramics combine the properties of glass and ceramics, providing durability under heat. Quartz glass excels in high-temperature applications due to its purity and low thermal expansion. Soda lime glass is the most common, used in windows and containers. Other specialty glasses cater to niche requirements.</p></p>
