<p><p>Surgical aspirator pumps are categorized into electric and pneumatic types based on their power source. Electric aspirator pumps use electrical energy to create suction, offering consistent performance and ease of use in various surgical applications. In contrast, pneumatic aspirator pumps rely on compressed air for suction, making them suitable for environments where electricity is limited or unsafe. Both types are essential for effectively removing bodily fluids during surgical procedures, enhancing patient safety and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
