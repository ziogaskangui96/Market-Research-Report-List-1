<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) insurance market is segmented into two main types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, requiring NEV owners to have basic coverage for third-party liability, ensuring financial protection in case of accidents. Optional insurance allows owners to choose additional coverage options, such as comprehensive, collision, or theft protection, enhancing their security against various risks. Together, these insurance types cater to the diverse needs of NEV owners while promoting safer driving practices.</p></p>
