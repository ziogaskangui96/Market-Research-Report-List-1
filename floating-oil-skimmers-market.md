<p><p>The floating oil skimmers market consists of various types, primarily including belt oil skimmers and disc oil skimmers. Belt oil skimmers use a continuous loop of absorbent material that captures oil as it moves through water, making them effective for large spills. Disc oil skimmers feature rotating discs that collect oil from the water's surface, offering efficient performance in smaller areas. Other types may include vacuum skimmers or brush skimmers, each designed for specific applications and oil recovery needs in diverse environments.</p></p>
