<p><p>The thermal management materials market for microelectronics comprises various types, each serving specific cooling needs. Conductive pastes are used for efficient heat dissipation and electrical connectivity. Conductive tapes offer easy application and flexibility for thermal interface. Phase change materials transition between solid and liquid at certain temperatures, optimizing thermal performance. Gap fillers conform to surfaces, filling voids to enhance thermal transfer. Thermal greases improve contact between components, reducing thermal resistance for optimal device performance and reliability.</p></p>
