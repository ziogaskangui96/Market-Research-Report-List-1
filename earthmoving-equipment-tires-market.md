<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Earthmoving Equipment Tires market is primarily divided into radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature a steel belt construction, offering superior durability, improved traction, and better heat dissipation, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, bias tires consist of layers of fabric crossing at angles, providing greater sidewall strength and flexibility, which can enhance load-carrying capacity. Each type serves distinct operational needs, influencing performance, lifespan, and maintenance costs in earthmoving activities.</p></p>
