<p><p>Screen magnifier market types include cloud-based and web-based solutions, each designed to enhance accessibility. Cloud-based screen magnifiers operate remotely, allowing users to access magnification tools via the internet, providing flexibility across devices. They typically require a subscription and offer features like synchronization between devices. In contrast, web-based screen magnifiers function directly through browsers, enabling users to magnify content without installing software. Both types facilitate improved visibility for users with visual impairments, enhancing their online experiences and productivity.</p></p>
