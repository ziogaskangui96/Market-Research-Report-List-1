<p><p>PCB AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) equipment is categorized into inline and offline markets based on their integration within the production process. Inline AOI systems are integrated directly into the manufacturing line, providing real-time inspection during assembly to enhance efficiency and reduce defects. Conversely, offline AOI systems operate independently, allowing for batch inspections after production.
