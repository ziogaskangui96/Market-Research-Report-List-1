<p><strong>The Tilt Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Pendulum</li><li>Liquid Pendulum</li><li>Gas Pendulum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tilt sensors market is categorized into three main types: solid pendulum, liquid pendulum, and gas pendulum sensors. Solid pendulum sensors utilize a rigid mass that tilts to measure inclination, providing high stability and sensitivity. Liquid pendulum sensors employ a liquid-filled chamber, where the liquid's tilt is measured to gauge angle changes, suitable for dynamic applications. Gas pendulum sensors use gas-filled chambers to sense tilt through pressurization changes, offering versatility in harsh environments. Each type serves specific industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
