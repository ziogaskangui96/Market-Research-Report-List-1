<p><p>The Silver Reinforced Glass Ionomer Cement market is segmented into Light Cure and Self Cure types. Light Cure cement utilizes a curing light to initiate the hardening process, offering rapid setting and enhanced control during application. In contrast, Self Cure cement hardens chemically without the need for light, making it ideal for areas where exposure to light is limited or impractical. Both types are valued for their aesthetic qualities, adhesion, and fluoride release, making them popular in dental restorative procedures.</p></p>
