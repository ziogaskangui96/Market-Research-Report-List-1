<p><p>The Gem Microscope market is divided into two primary types: monocular and multi-eye microscopes. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for individual users, ideal for basic observations and educational settings. In contrast, multi-eye microscopes are equipped with two or more eyepieces, allowing multiple users to view samples simultaneously. This design enhances collaborative learning and detailed analysis, making it suitable for research institutions and professional environments where shared observations are essential.</p></p>
