<p><p>The Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) skylights market includes two primary types: thin film solar panels and crystalline solar panels. Both types enhance energy generation while maintaining aesthetic appeal, creating sustainable building solutions through integration into skylight structures.</p></p>
