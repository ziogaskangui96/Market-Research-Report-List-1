<p><strong>The PM2.5 Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PM2.5 monitoring equipment market comprises various types, including TEOM monitors and Beta Attenuation Monitors, among others. TEOM monitors utilize a tapered element to collect particulate matter, providing real-time mass concentration readings. Beta Attenuation Monitors measure the attenuation of beta radiation as it passes through collected PM2.5, allowing for effective quantification of particulate pollution. Other monitoring equipment may include optical monitors, gravimetric samplers, and mobile air quality sensors, each serving distinct purposes in assessing air quality and compliance.</p></p>
