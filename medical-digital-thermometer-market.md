<p><p>The medical digital thermometer market primarily consists of two main types: electronic and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers use electronic sensors to measure body temperature accurately, providing readings within minutes, and are commonly used for oral, rectal, or underarm measurements. Infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure body temperature from a distance by detecting infrared radiation emitted from the body, making them ideal for quick assessments, especially in clinical and public health settings, as they minimize cross-contamination.</p></p>
