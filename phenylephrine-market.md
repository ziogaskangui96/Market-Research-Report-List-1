<p><p>The phenylephrine market is segmented into three administration types: intramuscular, intravenous, and intravenous drip. Intramuscular phenylephrine is injected into muscle tissue, providing rapid absorption and systemic effects. Intravenous phenylephrine delivers the drug directly into the bloodstream, allowing for immediate action, often used in critical care settings. Intravenous drip involves a continuous infusion of phenylephrine, ensuring sustained therapeutic levels over time, which is beneficial for managing persistent hypotension. Each type caters to specific medical scenarios and patient needs.</p></p>
