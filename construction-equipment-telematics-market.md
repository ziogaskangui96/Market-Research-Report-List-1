<p><strong>The Construction Equipment Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular</li><li>Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction equipment telematics market comprises two primary types: cellular and satellite. Cellular telematics utilizes mobile networks to transmit data, offering real-time tracking and monitoring within areas with strong cellular coverage. This option is often cost-effective and suited for urban environments. In contrast, satellite telematics provides global coverage, functioning in remote locations where cellular signals may be weak or nonexistent. This technology is critical for tracking equipment across vast job sites or in off-the-grid areas, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
