<p><p>The active infrared sensors market is primarily divided into two types: thermal and quantum sensors. Quantum infrared sensors, on the other hand, utilize advanced materials to detect infrared light at different wavelengths, offering high sensitivity and resolution. Both types serve diverse industries, including security, automotive, and healthcare, enabling precise detection and measurement in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
