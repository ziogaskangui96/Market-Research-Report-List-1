<p><ul><li>Mechanical Dehumidifier</li><li>Electronic Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential dehumidifier market is primarily categorized into mechanical and electronic dehumidifiers. Mechanical dehumidifiers, commonly using refrigerant technology, extract moisture from the air by cooling it, which condenses and collects water. They are typically more energy-efficient at lower temperatures. In contrast, electronic dehumidifiers utilize thermoelectric technology to eliminate humidity without moving parts, often operating silently and compactly. While mechanical units are effective in larger areas, electronic models are suitable for smaller spaces, offering various energy consumption profiles and functionalities.</p></p>
