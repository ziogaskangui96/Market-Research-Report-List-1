<p><p>The automotive flush door handles market includes two main types: spiral hidden door handles and translational hidden door handles. Spiral hidden door handles feature a rotating mechanism that allows the handle to appear flush with the vehicle's surface when not in use, enhancing aerodynamics and aesthetics. Translational hidden door handles operate by sliding or moving horizontally to reveal the handle, providing a sleek, modern look. Both types aim to combine style, functionality, and improved vehicle design.</p></p>
