<p><strong>The Flame Arrestors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>End-of-line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame arrestors are safety devices designed to prevent the propagation of flames in industrial systems. The In-line flame arrestor is installed directly in the pipeline and is essential for protecting against internal explosions by allowing gas flow while blocking flames. The End-of-line flame arrestor, positioned at the termination of a piping system, prevents external flames from entering the system. Both types are critical in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals to ensure safe operations and mitigate fire risks.</p></p>
