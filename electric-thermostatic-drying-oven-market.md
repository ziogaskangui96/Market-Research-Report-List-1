<p><p>The Electric Thermostatic Drying Oven market features two primary types: Blast Drying Ovens and Vacuum Drying Ovens. Blast Drying Ovens utilize forced air circulation to achieve rapid and uniform heating, making them ideal for general drying applications. In contrast, Vacuum Drying Ovens operate under reduced pressure, allowing for lower temperature drying, which preserves sensitive materials and prevents oxidation. Both types are essential in laboratories and industries for effectively removing moisture from various samples and products.</p></p>
