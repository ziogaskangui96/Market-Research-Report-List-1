<p><p>The discrete devices and power modules market comprises various semiconductor components essential for power management and conversion. IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) is used for high-voltage applications, while MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) is preferred for low-voltage switching. Diodes ensure unidirectional current flow, crucial in rectification. Intelligent Power Modules integrate multiple functions, enhancing efficiency and control. Si/SiC hybrid modules combine silicon and silicon carbide technologies, offering improved thermal performance and efficiency, especially in high-power and high-temperature applications.</p></p>
